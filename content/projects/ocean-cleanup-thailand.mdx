## üê† Project Activities

### Beach Cleanup Operations
Daily organized cleanups along Phuket and Phi Phi Island beaches, removing plastic waste and debris that threatens marine life.

### Coral Restoration Program
Participate in coral gardening and reef restoration activities, helping to rebuild damaged coral ecosystems.

### Marine Life Monitoring
Assist marine biologists in tracking sea turtle nesting sites and monitoring fish populations.

### Community Education
Engage with local schools and communities to raise awareness about marine conservation.
