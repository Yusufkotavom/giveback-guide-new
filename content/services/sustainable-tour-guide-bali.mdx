## 📋 Frequently Asked Questions

### Is this suitable for children?
Yes! Our tours are family-friendly and educational. Children under 12 receive a 50% discount. We can adapt activities to be age-appropriate.

### What if it rains?
Bali's weather is unpredictable, but we operate rain or shine. Indoor activities like workshops and cultural exchanges continue as planned. We provide umbrellas and rain protection.

### Do I need to be physically fit?
Most activities require only basic fitness. The rice terrace portion involves some walking on uneven terrain. Please inform us of any mobility concerns when booking.

### Are vegetarian/vegan options available?
Absolutely! Balinese cuisine offers many plant-based dishes. Please inform us of dietary requirements when booking.
