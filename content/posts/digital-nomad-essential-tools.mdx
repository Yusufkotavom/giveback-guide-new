## ğŸ’¡ Pro Tips for Tool Management

### Organization Strategy

2. **Use Cloud Sync** for consistent access across devices
3. **Set Up Shortcuts** for frequently used apps
4. **Regular Cleanup** - remove unused tools monthly

- **Use Free Tiers** strategically for testing
- **Share Team Plans** with trusted nomad friends
