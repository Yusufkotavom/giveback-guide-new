## 🎯 Country-Specific Tips

### Thailand
- **Best months**: Nov-Mar (cool season)
- **Budget**: $20-25/day
- **Don't miss**: Islands, temples, street food

### Vietnam
- **Best months**: Mar-May, Sep-Nov
- **Budget**: $15-20/day
- **Don't miss**: Ha Long Bay, motorbike tours

### Cambodia
- **Best months**: Nov-Mar
- **Budget**: $15-20/day
- **Don't miss**: Angkor Wat, floating villages

### Laos
- **Best months**: Nov-Mar
- **Budget**: $15-20/day
- **Don't miss**: Mekong River, waterfalls

## 💡 Pro Budget Tips

<blockquote class="border-l-4 border-green-500 pl-4 italic text-gray-600 my-4">
"The best experiences in Asia often cost nothing - watching sunsets, chatting with locals, exploring temples, and walking through markets."
</blockquote>

### Daily Savings Strategies
1. **Eat where locals eat** - follow the crowds
2. **Walk when possible** - most cities are walkable
3. **Buy water in bulk** - refill smaller bottles
4. **Use public transport** - avoid tourist taxis
5. **Stay longer** - weekly/monthly discounts

### Free Activities
- Temple visits (mostly free)
- Beach time
- Hiking trails
- Local markets
- Sunset viewing spots
