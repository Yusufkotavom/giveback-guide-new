- **Layers**: Daytime can be warm (25°C) but mornings cold
- **Footwear**: Sturdy shoes untuk trekking dan horseback riding
- **Photography**: Extra batteries (cold weather drains fast)
- **Health**: Personal medications, altitude sickness remedies
